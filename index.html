Hi !

I sometimes spend a lot of time on Google Map, just travelling around (especially durnig lockdowns). But MapboxGl also offers interesting renderings with DEM and buildings extrusion.
So this page was just a test project, but I decided to make profitable all this time spent on maps by trying to archive here some cool places.

Just click on the "Fly" button to start the trip.
The list of places you can discover here is stored as a JSON in the data folder.
To simplify adding new locations to the JSON I made the "Collect coordinates" button. If you find other cool spots, click the button and the location coordinates will be displayed, ready to be copied pasted into the JSON.

Some places use Mapbox DEM, rendering may take a little while for views that use pitch and bearing
